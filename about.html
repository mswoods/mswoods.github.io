About me...

I am a Senior Specialized Solutions Architect with Amazon Web Services. With over 20 years of experience in technology, broadcast and digital media, I've spent my career helping customers design and deploy broadcast and video streaming workflows. Recent highlights include working to launch the first non-commercial consumer ultra-high definition (UHD) channel in North America (NASA TV UHD), and architecting the first ever live 4K video stream from the International Space Station at the 2017 NAB Show. Prior to joining AWS Elemental, I held positions with Harmonic, Envivio, and Ericsson (formally Tandberg Television). Check out some of the cool projects I've worked on below.

Live 4K from Space

A History First - NASA produces the first ever live 4K video stream from space during a Super Session at the 2017 NAB Show using AWS technology.

Fireworks at the Fountain

In an unprecedented digital and broadcast event, WKMG-TV News 6 produced more than 100 hours of livestream content for ClickOrlando.com during the station’s annual coverage of the Lake Eola “Fireworks at the Fountain” on July 4.
